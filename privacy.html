Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Flickr (flickr-site.vercel.app) is a free movie discovery tool that helps you find films, view ratings, watch trailers, and find where to legally stream them. We do not host or stream any movies ourselves.

What information we collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. We do not require you to create an account or log in. However, third-party services used on this site may collect data automatically:

  • Google AdSense — serves ads on this site and uses cookies to show you relevant advertisements based on your browsing history and interests.
  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — powers our movie search and data. Requests are made to their API when you search or browse movies.
  • YouTube — trailers are embedded via YouTube. When you play a trailer, YouTube may collect data per their own privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address.

Cookies

This site uses cookies through Google AdSense to serve personalised advertisements. These cookies track your browsing behaviour across websites to show you relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads by visiting Google's ad settings.

How we use your data

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal data. The only data processing that occurs on this site is handled by the third-party services listed above, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy (covers AdSense, Fonts, YouTube)
  • TMDB Privacy Policy

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete data held about you by the third-party services above. Please refer to their individual privacy policies for how to exercise these rights.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

Questions about this privacy policy? This site is an independent project — for any concerns, please refer to the third-party service policies linked above.
